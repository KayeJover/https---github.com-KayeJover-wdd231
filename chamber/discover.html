Welcome to Discover Page

Demographics

  • Founded: 1903
  • Membership: Over 800 active members, ranging from small and medium enterprises (SMEs) to large corporations.
  • Industries Represented: Manufacturing, tourism, real estate, IT-BPO, agriculture, and service industries.
  • Location: Cebu City, the capital of Cebu province and a central hub of business and trade in the Visayas region.

Statistical Data

  • Cebu’s Population: Approximately 5 million (as of 2023)
  • GDP Contribution: Cebu contributes significantly to the country's GDP, being the largest economic center in the Visayas region.
  • Tourism Statistics: Cebu is one of the top tourist destinations in the Philippines, attracting over 2 million visitors annually (pre-pandemic data).

Key Events

  • Cebu Business Month (CBM): An annual month-long event that focuses on fostering entrepreneurship, innovation, and business networking.
  • Grand Chamber Awards: An annual recognition event that honors outstanding local businesses and entrepreneurs in Cebu.
  • CCCI General Membership Meeting: Regular meetings held by the Chamber for members to discuss business trends, updates, and opportunities.

Attractions in Cebu

  • Magellan’s Cross: A historical landmark symbolizing the arrival of Christianity in the Philippines.
  • Kawasan Falls: A famous natural attraction for trekking and canyoneering located in the southwestern part of Cebu.
  • Cebu IT Park: A major hub for IT-BPO companies, where many CCCI member businesses operate.
